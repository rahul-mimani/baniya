BillMaker MIT · open source

Billing for small shops, built to run on nothing.

An offline-first billing & invoicing system: a mobile app your staff use to make bills with or without signal, an admin portal to manage catalog and customers, and a client portal where customers view their own bills. It syncs through Firebase, Supabase and Cloudflare — and self-hosts on free tiers.

View on GitHub → Capacity & scale

What's inside

Mobile app Capacitor

The billing app staff use. Local-first — works fully offline, syncs when back online. Ships as an Android APK.

auth-service Cloudflare

A Worker handling OTP login, sessions, and the cron that mirrors Firestore to a queryable replica.

Web portal Vercel

One app, two roles: an admin console for the shop, and a client portal where customers see bills & deals.

Bring your own backend no lock-in

Point it at your own Firebase & Supabase projects. No secrets in source; every value is your own config.
